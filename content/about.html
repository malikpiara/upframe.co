We believe ideas can change the world.

And that anyone can produce an impact.

Upframe aims to become a global reference and authority in pre-acceleration, shaping the next generation of entrepreneurs by building an immersive experience focused on young aspiring startup builders.

We want to empower a generation of entrepreneurs with a critical mindset and tools to follow their dreams, connecting them to the people and institutions who will smooth their journey.

It's not our expectation that everyone who comes through our doors will already have a startup but it is our expectation that by the time they finish with us that they know that a startup dream is possible and plausible.

Growth Mindset

We are always and consistently learning, analysing and understanding what went well and what could have gone better. We are a 'work in progress', always growing through the result of previous smaller steps.

Disruption

We embrace our true personality, being authentic in the way we communicate. We are not afraid to go after our dreams and objectives, solving the problems we set ourselves to work on, no matter how hard or crazy the solutions might be.

Sharing

Knowledge must be shared. If we work together, we can built build a stronger future for our country and the following generations, shaping experiences with the objective of promoting a more open and united society.

To make this dream possible we have to thank Startup Lisboa and our mentors.

Being Startup Lisboa Official Pre-Acceleration Programme sets a high bar for quality, dedication and professionalism that the most influential Portuguese incubator has been constantly pushing up throughout their existence.

In addition to their expertise we are supported by a group of wonderful and experienced mentors who passed though companies like Tesla, Twitter and Spotify, and many more.
